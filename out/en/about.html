Our Timeline in AGS

Discover our timeline at AGS and learn about our most notable milestones, exploring our achievements and advances in the airport services industry.

February 2019

AVIATION GROUND SOLUTIONS IS FOUNDED

Aviation Grounds Solution (AGS) was founded in Guatemala. Since its inception, AGS was prepared to offer FBO (Fixed Base Operator) and Ground Handling services, meeting the needs of aircraft on the ground, providing logistical support, fuel services, and other essential facilities for aviation.

March 2019

FIRST FLIGHT OPERATION

The first flight operation marked a significant and defining milestone for the company. During this operation, AGS demonstrated its ability to manage and coordinate all aspects related to the ground handling of an aircraft, including fuel services, logistical support, and attention to the needs of the crew and passengers, ensuring an efficient and safe operation.
